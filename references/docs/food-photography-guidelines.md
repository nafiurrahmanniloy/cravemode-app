# Food Photography Guidelines for FoodShot AI

Best practices for AI-enhanced restaurant food photography that drives engagement.

---

## Image Enhancement Principles

### 1. Lighting
- **Natural Light**: Bright, diffused daylight is ideal
- **Warm Tones**: Add golden hour warmth for appetizing feel
- **Avoid**: Harsh shadows, cold fluorescent tones
- **Enhancement**: Boost highlights, soften shadows, add rim lighting

### 2. Color Grading
- **Vibrant but Natural**: Enhance saturation without over-processing
- **Food-Specific**:
  - Red meats: Rich browns with slight red boost
  - Greens: Vibrant but not neon
  - Sauces: Deep, glossy appearance
- **Background**: Slightly desaturated to make food pop

### 3. Composition
- **Rule of Thirds**: Place focal point off-center
- **Negative Space**: Don't overcrowd the frame
- **Angles**:
  - **Overhead (90°)**: Flat lays, multiple dishes, brunch spreads
  - **45° Angle**: Most versatile, shows depth and height
  - **Eye Level**: Burgers, sandwiches, tall items
- **Depth of Field**: Shallow focus on main dish, blur background

### 4. Styling Enhancements
- **Steam & Motion**: Add visual steam, sauce pours, garnish drops
- **Freshness Cues**: Water droplets, glistening surfaces, melting cheese
- **Garnishes**: Fresh herbs, microgreens, sauce drizzles
- **Props**: Minimal — forks, napkins, ingredient hints

## Style Presets

### "Bright & Fresh"
- **Use For**: Salads, smoothie bowls, breakfast, healthy options
- **Settings**: High brightness, vibrant greens/yellows, airy background
- **Mood**: Clean, energetic, healthy

### "Dark & Moody"
- **Use For**: Fine dining, steaks, cocktails, desserts
- **Settings**: Low-key lighting, deep shadows, rich colors
- **Mood**: Luxurious, intimate, sophisticated

### "Casual & Authentic"
- **Use For**: Burgers, pizza, street food, comfort food
- **Settings**: Natural lighting, realistic colors, visible texture
- **Mood**: Approachable, crave-worthy, unpretentious

### "Luxury Fine Dining"
- **Use For**: High-end restaurants, tasting menus, plated desserts
- **Settings**: Perfect plating, subtle lighting, elegant backgrounds
- **Mood**: Refined, artistic, exclusive

## Format Specifications

### 9:16 (Vertical - Reels/Stories)
- **Best For**: Instagram Reels, TikTok, Stories
- **Composition**: Vertical hero shot, text-safe areas at top/bottom
- **Framing**: Tighter crop, focus on dish

### 1:1 (Square - Feed Posts)
- **Best For**: Instagram feed, carousel posts
- **Composition**: Centered or rule of thirds
- **Framing**: Balanced, shows context (table setting)

### 16:9 (Horizontal - YouTube)
- **Best For**: YouTube thumbnails, website headers
- **Composition**: Wide angle, multiple dishes or setting
- **Framing**: Cinematic, shows restaurant atmosphere

## Common Issues & Fixes

| Issue | Enhancement Solution |
|-------|---------------------|
| Dim/dark photo | Boost exposure, add fill light, warm color temp |
| Washed out colors | Increase saturation, add contrast, deepen blacks |
| Busy background | Blur background, desaturate surroundings, crop tighter |
| Unappetizing color | Adjust white balance, enhance food-specific hues |
| Flat/lifeless | Add depth with shadows, highlights, texture clarity |
| Blurry/soft | Sharpen details, enhance texture, add micro-contrast |

## AI Prompt Patterns for Food

### Enhancement Prompts (for existing photos)
```
Enhance this [dish type] photo: [style preset]. Improve lighting to [bright/moody],
boost [specific colors], add [steam/glistening/freshness cues], blur background slightly.
Maintain realistic food texture. Output: [format].
```

### Generation Prompts (creating new compositions)
```
[Aspect ratio]. Professional food photography of [dish description].
[Angle]. [Lighting description]. [Style preset]. [Props/garnishes].
Restaurant setting, [mood adjective], appetizing, high-resolution.
```

```
Enhance this burger photo: Casual & Authentic style. Improve natural lighting,
boost golden browns and vibrant greens, add slight steam from patty,
```

```
Restaurant setting, crave-worthy, appetizing, 4K resolution.
```

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering to client:

- [ ] **Focus**: Main dish is sharp and clear
- [ ] **Lighting**: Balanced, no harsh shadows or blown highlights
- [ ] **Colors**: Vibrant but natural, appetizing tones
- [ ] **Composition**: Well-framed, proper negative space
- [ ] **Background**: Not distracting, complements dish
- [ ] **Texture**: Visible and appetizing (crispy, juicy, smooth, etc.)
- [ ] **Format**: Correct aspect ratio for client's needs
- [ ] **Resolution**: High-quality, no pixelation or artifacts
- [ ] **Consistency**: Matches client's brand style
